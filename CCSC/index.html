About us

Champions Community School Complex has been the branchild of Evangelist Kumi Duodu for many years. The concept of CCSC comes from the innate drive to help others who are: vulnerable, abandoned and been dubbed,"a burden to society".

Champions Community School Complex is a ministry of House of Champions Ministries (Champions Community Chapel) and operates under the authority of the church Mission-the Education Council, which is also under authority of the church. The school is guided by and transforms in general to the doctrine and practice of the church.

We envision Champions Community School Complex graduates who have a Biblical worldview that encompasses all of life and who are equipped and eager to take their place as servant leaders in the world. As classically educated men and women, CCSC graduates will possess broad knowledge, true wisdom, a capacity for clear reasoning, and the ability to communicate truth persuasively. In every area of life, they will recognize, appreciate, and spread truth, goodness, and beauty to the glory of God.

What we do

Feeding

All OVCs need food to function effectively. The major drive of most street orphans and orphans in labour is basically on how to acquire one or two square meals daily. HCM provides free feeding to our consumers to offload that burden to strongly position their focus in the main areas of human development for sustainablelife. HCM has a canteen service that cooks and serves all consumers nutritious meals to support their strength.

Clothing

HCM provides clothes to our consumers. These include those that would be used daily, uniforms for school, clothes for church service, sport wear and night wear.

Housing

All OVCs admitted into the education stream are given full housing facility. This is a dormitory constructed on the premises of the educational complex to facilitate easy movement of consumers to and from their needed post.
